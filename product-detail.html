Product Description

The iPhone 16 Pro represents the pinnacle of smartphone technology with its exceptional combination of power, camera capabilities, and seamless user experience. Designed with premium materials and cutting-edge features, this device sets a new standard for what a smartphone can do.

A17 Pro Chip

Experience unprecedented speed and efficiency with the A17 Pro chip, built on 3-nanometer technology. This advanced chip delivers up to 20% faster performance than the previous generation while maintaining impressive energy efficiency for all-day battery life.

Pro Camera System

Capture stunning photos and videos with the advanced triple-camera system featuring a 48MP main camera, 12MP ultra-wide, and 12MP telephoto with 5x optical zoom. The new Photonic Engine enhances image quality in mid to low-light conditions, while the improved Night mode delivers exceptional clarity in the darkest environments.

ProMotion XDR Display

Enjoy breathtaking visuals on the 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR display with ProMotion technology that automatically adjusts the refresh rate up to 120Hz for ultra-smooth scrolling and responsiveness. The Always-On display allows you to glance at important information without waking your device.

All-Day Battery Life

Stay powered throughout your day with the long-lasting battery that supports up to 23 hours of video playback. When you need to recharge, fast charging capability gives you up to 50% charge in around 30 minutes with a 20W adapter (sold separately).

Titanium Design

The iPhone 16 Pro features a sophisticated design with a titanium frame that's both lighter and stronger than previous models. The Ceramic Shield front cover provides four times better drop protection, while the textured matte glass back enhances grip and resists fingerprints.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the iPhone 16 Pro water resistant?

Yes, the iPhone 16 Pro has an IP68 rating, which means it is water resistant up to 6 meters for 30 minutes. However, this doesn't mean it is completely waterproof. Water resistance may decrease over time with normal wear, and liquid damage is not covered under warranty.

Does the iPhone 16 Pro come with a charger?

No, the iPhone 16 Pro does not come with a charger in the box. It comes with a USB-C to USB-C cable only. You will need to purchase a compatible charger separately. Apple recommends using a 20W or higher USB-C power adapter for fast charging.

Can I use my old iPhone cases with the iPhone 16 Pro?

It depends on which iPhone model you're upgrading from. The iPhone 16 Pro has slightly different dimensions compared to previous models. Cases designed specifically for the iPhone 15 Pro may fit, but they might not provide a perfect fit. For the best protection, we recommend using cases specifically designed for the iPhone 16 Pro.

What's the difference between the iPhone 16 Pro and the iPhone 16 Pro Max?

The main differences between the iPhone 16 Pro and iPhone 16 Pro Max are the screen size, battery capacity, and price. The iPhone 16 Pro has a 6.1-inch display, while the Pro Max has a larger 6.7-inch display. The Pro Max also has a bigger battery that provides longer battery life. Both models share the same processor, camera system, and other features. The Pro Max is more expensive due to its larger screen and battery.

Is 128GB storage enough for the iPhone 16 Pro?

Whether 128GB is enough depends on your usage. If you take a lot of photos and videos or download many apps and games, you might find 128GB limiting. The iPhone 16 Pro can shoot in ProRAW format, which produces large file sizes, and 4K videos also take up significant space. For most average users, 128GB should be sufficient, especially if you use cloud storage. However, if you're a heavy user who creates a lot of content, consider the 256GB or higher storage options.

Does the iPhone 16 Pro support 5G?

Yes, the iPhone 16 Pro supports 5G connectivity, including both sub-6GHz and mmWave bands in applicable countries. This allows for faster download and upload speeds when connected to a 5G network. The actual 5G performance may vary based on your carrier and location.
