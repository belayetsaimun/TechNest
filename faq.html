Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, returns, and more.

Shopping

How do I know if a product is in stock?

All products displayed on our website are currently in stock unless marked as "Out of Stock". The inventory is updated in real-time, so what you see is what's available. If a product is out of stock, you can click the "Notify Me" button to receive an email when it's available again.

Can I purchase items in bulk for my business?

Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders. If you're interested in placing a bulk order for your business, please contact our business sales team at business@technest.com with details about your requirements, and we'll provide you with a customized quote.

How can I be sure I'm buying an authentic product?

TechNest is an authorized retailer for all brands we sell. We source our products directly from manufacturers or their authorized distributors, ensuring every item is 100% authentic. Each product comes with the manufacturer's warranty and all original accessories. You can verify the authenticity of your purchase through the manufacturer's website using the serial number provided with your product.

Do you offer price matching?

Yes, we offer price matching on identical products from authorized retailers. If you find a lower price on the same product from a qualified competitor within 7 days of your purchase, we'll match the price. To request a price match, contact our customer service team with proof of the competitor's price.

Orders & Shipping

How long will it take to receive my order?

Orders within Dhaka are typically delivered within 2-3 business days. Orders to other locations in Bangladesh take 3-5 business days. Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email so you can monitor the progress of your delivery. Please note that delivery times may be extended during high-volume periods like holiday seasons or promotions.

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by:

  1. Logging into your TechNest account and viewing your order history
  2. Clicking the tracking link in the shipment confirmation email
  3. Contacting our customer service team with your order number
Our system sends automatic updates at each stage of the shipping process, so you'll always know where your order is.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only offer shipping within Bangladesh. We are working on expanding our shipping services to other countries in the future. Follow our social media channels or subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated on any changes to our shipping policies.

Can I change or cancel my order after it's been placed?

Orders can be modified or canceled within 1 hour of placement. After that, our system begins processing orders for shipping, making changes difficult. To request a change or cancellation, contact our customer service team immediately at support@technest.com or call our hotline at +8801581448561 with your order number ready.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most products in their original condition and packaging. Some exceptions apply, such as personalized items and opened software. To initiate a return, log into your account and select the order you wish to return, or contact our customer service team. Return shipping costs are covered by TechNest for defective items or our errors, otherwise, return shipping is the responsibility of the customer.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-7 business days
  • Mobile Banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket): 2-3 business days
  • Store Credit: Immediate
You'll receive an email notification when your refund has been processed.

Can I exchange a product instead of returning it?

Yes, we offer exchanges for items in their original condition. You can request an exchange for a different color, size, or even a different product of equal or greater value (paying the difference if applicable). To request an exchange, contact our customer service team with your order number and details of the item you'd like to exchange.

What should I do if I receive a defective product?

If you receive a defective product, please contact our customer service team within 48 hours of delivery. We'll need photos or videos showing the defect and the product's serial number. For electronic devices, our technical team may provide troubleshooting steps before authorizing a return. Once verified, we'll arrange for the item to be picked up at no cost to you and send a replacement or issue a full refund based on your preference.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard)
  • Mobile Banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket)
  • Cash on Delivery (for orders within Bangladesh)
  • Bank Transfer (for bulk orders)
All online payments are secured with industry-standard encryption to protect your information.

Is it safe to use my credit card on your website?

Yes, our website uses SSL encryption to protect your personal and payment information. We comply with PCI DSS requirements and never store complete credit card information on our servers. Additionally, we implement multiple security measures including fraud detection systems to ensure all transactions on our site are safe and secure.

When will my card be charged?

Your card will be authorized when you place your order and charged when your order ships. For pre-orders or back-ordered items, your card will be charged when the item is ready to ship. We will notify you via email before processing the charge for any orders that have been delayed more than 7 days.

Do you offer installment payment options?

Yes, we offer 0% interest installment payment options for purchases over ৳50,000 with selected banks. Currently, we have partnerships with City Bank, BRAC Bank, and Eastern Bank Limited (EBL) to offer 3, 6, or 12-month installment plans. To use this option, select "Pay with EMI" during checkout and choose your bank. Approval is subject to your bank's terms and conditions.

Account

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click on the account icon in the top right corner of any page
  2. Select "Create Account"
  3. Enter your email address and create a password
  4. Fill in your personal information
  5. Verify your email address by clicking the link in the verification email
Once verified, you can start shopping, track orders, and manage your account preferences.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  1. Click on the account icon in the top right corner
  2. Select "Sign In"
  3. Click "Forgot Password"
  4. Enter the email address associated with your account
  5. Check your email for a password reset link
  6. Follow the link to create a new password
If you don't receive the reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact our support team.

Can I shop as a guest without creating an account?

Yes, you can shop as a guest without creating an account. During checkout, you'll have the option to continue as a guest by providing only the necessary information to complete your order. However, creating an account offers several benefits, including order tracking, faster checkout in the future, access to exclusive offers, and the ability to review products you've purchased.

How can I update my account information?

To update your account information:

  1. Sign in to your TechNest account
  2. Click on "My Account" in the dropdown menu
  3. Select "Account Settings"
  4. Update your personal information, shipping addresses, or payment methods
  5. Click "Save Changes"
Your account will be immediately updated with the new information.

Warranty & Support

What warranty do you offer on products?

All products sold on TechNest come with the manufacturer's warranty. Additionally, we provide a TechNest Guarantee which offers:

  • Standard products: 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects
  • Premium TechNest-branded products: 2-year comprehensive warranty
  • Extended warranty options available at checkout for most electronics
Warranty coverage begins from the date of delivery. Specific warranty terms vary by product category and brand, which are listed on each product page.

How do I claim warranty service?

To claim warranty service:

  1. Log into your TechNest account and go to "Order History"
  2. Find the product and select "Request Warranty Service"
  3. Describe the issue you're experiencing
  4. Our support team will contact you within 24 hours with next steps
Alternatively, you can visit our service center in Dhaka with your purchase invoice and the product. For major items, we offer doorstep pickup and repair services in select locations.

What is not covered under warranty?

The following are typically not covered under warranty:

  • Physical damage caused by accidents, drops, or mishandling
  • Water damage or exposure to extreme conditions
  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Consumable parts (e.g., batteries after 6 months)
  • Software issues not related to hardware defects
  • Products with removed or damaged serial numbers
For specific warranty exclusions, please refer to the warranty information provided with your product or contact our customer service team.

Do you offer technical support for products?

Yes, we offer technical support for all products purchased from TechNest. Our support services include:

  • Phone support: Available daily from 9 AM to 10 PM
  • Email support: 24/7 with responses within 24 hours
  • Live chat: Available on our website from 10 AM to 8 PM
  • In-store support: Available at our physical locations during business hours
  • Video tutorials and guides on our website
For technical assistance, contact our support team at support@technest.com or call our hotline at +8801581448561.
